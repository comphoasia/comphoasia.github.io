ABOUT US

Com Pho Asia 2 provides you with the best tasting Vietnamese dishes in Barrie. From our classic pho to refreshing vermicelli bowls, to superb fried rice, we have everything your heart desires and more.


Our famous pho broth starts the previous day and is slowly perfected for a minimum of 10 hours before it's served piping hot. Our head chef painstakingly prepares the broth with our special spice mixture which includes cinnamon, cardamom, star anise, and many other secret spices.


Feast on the broth topped with a plethora of beef cuts and rice noodles or try our signature vermicelli bowl with grilled pork. Make sure you order a side of our crispy spring rolls with pork and shrimp!
